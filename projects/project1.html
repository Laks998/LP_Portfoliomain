SPORTSCOVE

A platform connecting sports learners with expert coaches through personalized training sessions and skill development programs.

I joined the team in March 2025, when the product was at the early wireframe stage. The foundation existed, but both UX and UI needed a complete rethink to create a cohesive, user-centered experience.

The Challenge

Transforming confusing wireframes into an intuitive sports learning platform

Complex User Flows

Booking and learning paths were convoluted, causing user drop-offs

Inconsistent UI

No design system existed, leading to visual inconsistencies

Accessibility

Lack of inclusive design practices limited usability for diverse users

My Impact

Key contributions that transformed the product

Streamlined complex flows, reducing the number of steps in various flows and improving completion rates.
