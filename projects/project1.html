SPORTSCOVE APP

Complete redesign including Design System from scratch

SPORTSCOVE

A platform connecting sports learners with expert coaches.

I joined the team in March 2025, when the product was at the early wireframe stage. The foundation existed, but both UX and UI needed a complete rethink.

The Problem

Existing wireframes had major usability challenges and inconsistent UI.

Task flows were confusing, and the design lacked a clear system. My goal: bring structure, usability, and delight.

My Role

What I did to improve the app:

  • Created a design system from scratch.
  • Built all UI components.
  • Redesigned task flows — some slightly, some completely.
  • Introduced guest mode.
  • Added subtle animations to enhance experience.
  • Built prototypes, conducted user testing, and iterated.
  • Did QA testing before launch.
