building quippy

After leaving Consultcraft in early October, I've been working full-time on Quippy—a browser extension for instant text conversions and lookups.

Launched it a month and a half ago for initial testing, and it's now live for everyone! Highlight text, press Ctrl+Shift+Q, and get definitions, currency conversions, time zones, calculations—all without breaking your flow.

Try it: Chrome Web Store
