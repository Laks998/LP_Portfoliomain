02

building quippy

My first LinkedIn post in years announced Quippy—a desktop widget I'm building. The idea came from constantly jumping to Google for small tasks: looking up words, converting currencies, quick calculations, checking time zones.

With Quippy, you type "?" or "Alt+Q" → highlight any text → get instant results without leaving what you're doing. Your own little Google, right at your fingertips.

Currently browser-only, but I'm vibe coding it into life right after finishing this portfolio.
